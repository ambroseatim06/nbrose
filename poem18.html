The Quantum Leap

By Ambrose Atim

A quantum leap in the world of thought,
Where particles dance and mysteries are caught.
In the realm of uncertainty, we find the way,
To glimpse the future and reshape the day.

Through qubits and quantum, a new dawn breaks,
transforming the world with each choice it makes.
In the quantum leap, we touch the divine,
where the future and present perfectly align.
