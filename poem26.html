Cybersecurity Watch

By Ambrose Atim

In the realm of cyberspace, vigilance reigns,
Cybersecurity watch, where safety gains.
Guardians of data, protecting the flow,
From threats and breaches that seek to sow.

In the shadows of networks, they stand alert,
Defending the realm from every covert hurt.
In the watchful eyes and the digital shield,
The safety of our world is steadfastly healed.
