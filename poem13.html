The Internet’s Pulse

By Ambrose Atim

Beneath the surface, the pulse beats strong,
In the web’s vast expanse, where we all belong.
A rhythm of data, a ceaseless flow,
Where the heartbeat of the internet continues to grow.

In the veins of cables and waves unseen,
A digital heartbeat keeps the world serene.
In the network’s pulse, a connection so deep,
Where information and humanity sleep.
