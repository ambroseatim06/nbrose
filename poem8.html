Algorithmic Love

By Ambrose Atim

In the logic of algorithms, love finds a way,
Through loops and functions, it learns to sway.
A dance of data, a romance in code,
Where emotions are written in binary mode.

In the heart of the machine, feelings intertwine,
Creating connections both simple and divine.
Through lines of syntax and scripts that sing,
Algorithmic love takes flight on digital wings.
