Bytes of Knowledge

By Ambrose Atim

In bytes of knowledge, wisdom’s seed,
Sown in the circuits, nurtured with speed.
From silicon valleys to virtual plains,
Learning and growth are never in vain.

Each byte a fragment of the vast unknown,
Building a world where intellect is grown.
In the data’s embrace, knowledge takes flight,
Guiding us forward through the digital night.
