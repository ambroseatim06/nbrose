The Circuit Board’s Heartbeat

By Ambrose Atim

Beneath the metal, in the circuit’s vein,
Pulses a rhythm, a steady refrain.
The heartbeat of a board, a silent song,
Guiding electrons where they belong.

Through screens and goggles, we transcend,
Into dimensions where rules bend.
In this digital landscape we chart,
Imagination knows no bounds or part.
